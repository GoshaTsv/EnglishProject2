My school day

I wake up at 7 am, and then i go brush my teeth. At around 7.30am, i go to school. When i am at school, i go down to put my coat there. Then i go to the classroom we have the first lesson in and talk with my friends. At 8 am, the bell rings and we go to the classroom. Our form tutor calls out our names and we reply, then the lesson goes as normal. At 8.45 am the lesson ends and we have a 10 minute break. After the second lesson, at 9.40 we have breakfast. We usually get porridge with chicken. Then we have the third lesson at 9.55-10.40, the fourth from 10.55 to 11.40, the fifth which lasts 45 minutes from 11.55, and the final 6th lesson from 12.55 to 13.40. Then we go get out coats and go home. We also get homework. On school days i do the homework around 17 o'clock, meanwhile on weekends i do it at around 12.00. Then i usually go play some games with my friends, then i go home and sleep.
